# The Chicken Who Learned Chess

Once upon a time, a chicken named Gertrude wandered into a chess club.

"Bawk," she said, knocking over a rook with her beak.

The grandmaster stared. "Madam, that is not a legal move."

Gertrude ate the rook.

"That is also not a legal move," he said.

She proceeded to eat every piece on the board, then laid an egg on e4.

The grandmaster studied the position for forty-five minutes before resigning.

"I have never seen the Sicilian Poultry Defense played so aggressively," he told reporters afterward.

Gertrude was unavailable for comment. She was already in the next room, eating a backgammon set.
